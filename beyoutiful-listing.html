About This Opportunity

BeYOUtiful Bath · Body · Burn isn't just a store — it's a community hub. Founded by Jessie after she made it her mission to create natural, clean beauty products, the brand has grown into one of Spokane's most beloved local wellness destinations.

The store partners with over 40 local PNW businesses, hosts community events like Plant Bingo nights, and maintains a loyal, engaged following of 9,600+ on Instagram. Customers don't just shop here — they come back, they bring friends, and they genuinely love and trust this brand.

Advertising here puts your brand in front of Spokane's most community-oriented, local-first consumers — in an environment where shopping local isn't just preferred, it's the whole point.
