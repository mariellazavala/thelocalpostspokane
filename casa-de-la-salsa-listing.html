About This Opportunity

Casa de la Salsa is Spokane's premier Latin dance social club, hosting recurring Salsa, Bachata, Merengue, and Cumbia events that draw a vibrant, engaged crowd of young professionals, couples, and community members.

These aren't passive audiences — attendees arrive excited, stay for 2–4 hours, and are fully present all evening. Your brand shows up in an environment of energy, celebration, and community trust. Sponsors are recognized as supporters of something people genuinely love and return to month after month.
