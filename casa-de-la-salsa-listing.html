About This Opportunity

Casa de la Salsa hosts recurring Latin dance socials across Spokane that draw a vibrant, engaged crowd of young professionals, couples, and community members for evenings of live music, dancing, and genuine connection.

These aren't passive audiences — attendees arrive excited, stay for hours, and are fully present throughout the event. Your brand shows up in an environment of energy, celebration, and community trust. Sponsors are recognized as supporters of something people genuinely love and come back to month after month.

📺 About screens & signage: Casa de la Salsa events rotate between venues across Spokane. Screen size and placement vary by venue — from large projected screens to TV monitors. Your sponsor materials will be displayed on whatever screen setup is available at each event. We'll confirm venue details with you after booking.
